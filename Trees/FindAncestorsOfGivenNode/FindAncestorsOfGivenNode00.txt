Given a binary tree, find all ancestors of given node in it.

 


For example, consider below binary tree

          1
        /   \
       /     \
      2       3
     / \     / \
    4   5   6   7
           /     \
          8       9


Ancestor of node 9 are 7, 3 and 1
Ancestor of node 6 are 3 and 1
Ancestor of node 5 are 2 and 1
. . .
